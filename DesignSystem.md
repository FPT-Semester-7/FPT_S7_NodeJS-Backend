# The MC Hub - Design System Overview

## Vibe & Aesthetics
- **Core Identity:** Professional, Modern, Trustworthy, Stage-Inspired.
- **Visual Style:** High-end, premium feel with a focus on spotlight effects and clear typography.

## Color Palette
- **Navy Blue (Primary - Trust/Authority):** `#002366` (or similar deep navy)
    - *Usage:* Navigation, Sidebar, Primary Buttons, Headers.
- **Gold/Yellow (Accent - Spotlight/Premium):** `#FFD700` / `#FDB813`
    - *Usage:* Verified Badges, Highlights, Booked Calendar States, Ratings.
- **Success/Verified Green:** `#28A745`
- **Error/Busy Red:** `#DC3545`

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** Inter or Roboto (Professional & Clean).
- **Secondary (Accents):** Playfair Display (For a touch of elegance in Stage titles).

## Global Components
### 1. Top Navigation
- **Logo:** High-contrast logo on the far left.
- **Search Bar:** Centered with glassmorphism effect.
- **Icons:** Notifications (with badge), Profile Dropdown, Help circle.

### 2. Sidebar (Consistent Navigation)
- **Items:** 
    - Dashboard (Home)
    - Script Library
    - My Bookings
    - Earnings
    - My Portfolio
    - Settings

## UI/UX Principles
- **Stage-Inspired Reading:** High contrast for MC scripts (Light Background, Dark Serif Text) for visibility under stage lights.
- **Micro-animations:** Subtle hover states on cards, smooth transitions between onboarding steps.
- **Feedback Loops:** Clear confirmation states (Success Checkmarks) for all major actions (Booking, KYC, Payment).
